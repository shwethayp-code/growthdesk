Why institutes switch

The CRM that actually understands JEE prep.

LeadSquared was built for B2B sales pipelines. Meritto for university admissions. Neither knows what a "PCM percentage" is, or why a Hot lead in March needs a different message than one in November.

GrowthDesk was built by a coaching institute, for coaching institutes. Every field, every score weight, every email template comes from a real lead pipeline running real students through real JEE seasons.

  • Score weights you can tune

    The default weights (PCM 30, Interest 25, Source 20, Income 15, Exam 10) are starting points. Edit them in the Apps Script in 90 seconds.

  • DPDP-compliant by default

    Every lead, every campaign, every market — auto-checked against India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. With audit log.

  • Owned, not rented

    One HTML file. Your data lives in your browser or your Google Sheet. No vendor can hold it hostage.
